Top 5 Desserts I Like

Chocolate lava cake with ice cream is such a good dessert. I adore the contrast between the hot gooey fudge and the cool ice cream.

I love ice cream in general, but mint chocolate chip is probably my favorite flavor. The coolness of the mint is perfect for a hot sunny day.

I like cookies in many varieties. They’re so simple, but so good.

I love gummies, whether they are sour gummy worms or gummy bears. I can’t explain why, but they are just a fun thing to eat.

It’s a crisp cookie with a mint filling, what’s not to love?
